# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

A comprehensive Human Resources Analytics dashboard built in **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze employee demographics, workforce distribution, and attrition patterns. The dashboard enables HR teams and management to monitor key workforce metrics, identify high-risk employee segments, and make data-driven decisions.

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide HR professionals with a centralized view of workforce metrics and employee attrition insights.

The dashboard helps answer questions such as:

- How many employees are currently active?
- What is the overall attrition rate?
- Which departments experience the highest attrition?
- Which salary groups are most affected?
- Which job roles have lower job satisfaction?
- How is attrition distributed across age groups, gender, and experience levels?

- Attrition by Department

- Age Group Distribution
- Attrition by Gender

- Department-wise Employee Count

---

### Interactive Filters

- Department
- Age Group

---

# 📊 Key Insights

- Overall employee attrition rate is **16.3%**.
- Employees in the **26–35 age group** form the largest workforce.
- Attrition varies significantly across departments.
- Employees within lower salary slabs experience comparatively higher attrition.
- Job satisfaction differs across various job roles, highlighting potential retention opportunities.
